About Telyra

For centuries, humanity has built its kingdom through peace and perseverance, guarding itself from the dark forces of magic and terrifying creatures that dwell beyond the reach of civilization. Sam, a young hunter, lives in solitude within the heart of the forest. One fateful night, his peaceful life is shattered when a brutal raid by trolls and orcs forces him to flee with nothing but his life. As Sam embarks on a journey to find safety, he remains unaware of a much greater threat brewing in the shadows. The fate of the entire realm now hinges on Sam embracing the hero he was always meant to become.

Gameplay

Telyra features a unique world-switching mechanic. The World Warp ability rotates special in-game areas by 180 degrees, transports the protagonist to another dimension, and opens up new locations to explore.


Combat

The Telyra combat system revolves around dynamic combos. The hero can engage enemies on the ground, launch them into the air, and continue the fight mid-flight. Armed with a quarterstaff and a hand fan, Mako is capable of both melee and ranged attacks.
